Power Law Tail Pricing
Calculate far out-of-the-money option prices using Taleb's power law formula. This tool estimates option prices at strike K2 given the known price at a nearby anchor strike K1, based on the assumption that tail risk follows a power law distribution.





Call Option Formula (Equation 6):
C(K2) = C(K1) × [(K2 - S0) / (K1 - S0)]^(1-α)

Where K1, K2 > S0 (OTM calls) and α > 1
